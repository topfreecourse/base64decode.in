Base64Decode

About Us

Base64Decode.in is a no-cost web tool designed to help developers, security experts, and general users decode Base64 data quickly and effectively.

We prioritize security and privacy — all decoding operations take place directly in your browser, with no data being sent to any external server.

Our tool supports multiple decoding formats and character sets including UTF-8, ASCII, UTF-16, and others. Advanced features like decoding by lines, URL-safe processing, MIME-style 76-character wrapping, support for LF or CRLF newlines, and live decoding can also be enabled by users.

We aim to deliver a fast, responsive, and clutter-free user experience across all devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile. Constant feedback from our users helps us refine and enhance our features.
